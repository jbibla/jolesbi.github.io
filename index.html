about me

i am a user experience and user interface designer with front-end development skills. i studied comparative religion and sustainability @concordia university and completed one year of multimedia design and web development courses @humber college.

i love riding my bicycle, eating fermented foods, and making unusual faces while i play the bass. i'm currently living in toronto, canada. if you're interested, here's my resume and my portfolio.

Silent Disco Squad

i worked with the silent-disco-squad team to design an interface for their forthcoming iOS and android apps. the result was a beautiful, simple, and functional digital interface.

the silent disco app will provide a listing of all active SDS communities worldwide and will have the ability to geo-locate the mobile dance parties as they traverse their respective urban environments. the app will also provide a platform for syncing the crowdsourced disco-mixes. the app is intended to support the creation of participant driven, public dance parties and will be available in app stores summer 2014.

CoinCulture

CoinCulture is a group of talented developers, marketers, and community builders based in guelph, ontario. the company manages a bitcoin ATM, organizes the guelph bitcoin meetups, participates in hackathons, and offers small business consulting for companies looking to integrate and understand the crypto-economy.

i worked with their team to take care of their branding, design, and front-end development needs. this included logo design, colour & typography, business cards, custom icons, and a fully responsive, browser friendly website.

* i'm also working on the ux and front-end design for yanlj - CoinCulture's exciting new wiki project *

Gardner Zuk Dessen

i was approached by Gardner Zuk Dessen, a well-established accounting firm, to design and build a professional, elegant, and responsive website. the site was built using zurb foundation and is fully responsive. it was a welcomed exercise in playful typography, whitespace, and tasteful simplicity.

Zach Gayne

i worked with Zach Gayne, a toronto based journalist, cinematographer, editor, and director to design and build a clean and cartoonish, responsive website. the site is built using zurb foundation and is fully responsive.

* i'm also working on the front-end design and development for Zach's coming web series 'Found Viral' *
